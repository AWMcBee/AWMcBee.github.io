Krabby Patty Infographic

School ProjectOctober 2017

Krabby Patty Secret Formula infographic was a fun school project where the challenge was to create an interesting infographic that people would want to look at. Using my skills in Adobe Illustrator, I was able to recreate SpongeBob's image in the solid color/infographic style.



Overall Design

The point of an infographic is to inform people in a graphical way. Duh. So I wanted to make sure the ingredients and instructions were easy to find and read. Placing the infomation in a circular manner created a nice flow that readers could follow. All that is left is to make it fun. Adding SpongeBob performing the tasks added a simple visual of what is being done in that task. The background bubbles added texture to the image as well as help guide the viewer through the poster. All in all, this was one of my earlier design projects that I'm still proud of today.
