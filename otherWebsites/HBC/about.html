About Us

Hodson’s Bay Company is a family owned and operated bicycle shop that serves the community. We offer a personalized shopping experience to help all ages and levels of riders find the best bicycle and accessories to fit their body and their type of riding. From trikes to tri-thalons, we have bikes for all riders. We have a large selection of road bikes, mountain bikes, commuters, children's bikes, BMX bikes, recumbents, fat tire bikes, and new electric bikes.


History

For over 40 years now, Hodson’s Bay has been providing the best product and service for our customers. Finding the perfects product for each and every customer no matter the age or skill level.
